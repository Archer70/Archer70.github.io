FizzBuzz

A small FizzBuzz example. 
fizz_buzz = FizzBuzz.new

# For individual numbers:
fizz_buzz.get_output_for 3
##> "fizz"
fizz_buzz.get_output_for 5
##> "buzz"
fizz_buzz.get_output_for 15
##> "fizz buzz"

# Or a sequence:
fizz_buzz.build_string 15
##> "1 2 fizz 4 buzz fizz 7 8 fizz buzz 11 fizz 13 14 fizz buzz"
